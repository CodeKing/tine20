Welcome to Tine 2.0

Tine 2.0 is a state-of-the-art open-source groupware solution providing e-mail, addressbook, calendar, crm and more just for free.

This project is the foundation of the commercial Tine 2.0 Business Edition which is provided by Metaways in Hamburg.

Building free software is actually a hard job and heavily relies on it's community, their contribution and their good will to improve Tine 2.0.

Join us

Help improving Tine 2.0! Every help is welcome and a good way to show off your skills and support for open-source software.

We need help at:

  • Testing and reporting issues
  • Help with translations if you are fluent in English and any other language
  • Grab an issue from our tracker and help fixing it
  • Got a nice idea for Tine 2.0? Write it down and start developing it
  • Experienced with Tine 2.0? Help other users at our forums

Interested in helping? Feel free to ask about help getting started at our board, other experienced users and developers are around willed to help you out!
